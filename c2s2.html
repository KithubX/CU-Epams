CHITKARA UNIVERSITY'S CULTURAL AND SOCIAL SERVICE CLUB (C2S2)

C2S2 club is open for all students of Chitkara University, Punjab Campus. The club functions not only during the working hours of the campus, it works even when the campus working hours are over. The club intends to encourage students to learn and teach activities that interests them. The vision of C2S2 is to create a community enriched with students who are enthusiastic and are keen to learn and teach new things.

The club is initiated in order to enable the students to reach their full potential as productive, caring and responsible citizens.


Mission

C2S2's mission is to help students unleash their potential in the co-curricular segments to foster, inculcate and nurture their hobbies. The c2s2 club will look after four significant fields like drama, dance, art and social activities.


The objectives of this society are:
  • To give ample opportunities to the students for self-expression.
  • To develop high standards of aesthetic sense, ethics, discipline and social spirit.
  • To develop harmonious growth of personality through a variety of co-curricular activities.
