EXPLORE 2017

It is with immense pleasure today that Chitkara University announces the advent of the next edition of one of the nation's largest known platforms for talent incubation and exhibition, EXPLORE'17.

Known for always holding its reputation for bringing energizing ideas to the audience , this year Chitkara University will pilot you into something amazingly inspiring , through the exciting theme of Explore'17. Reminiscing the glory of our past while envisioning the stirring possibilities of the future, Explore'15 is your channel to travel "Revivendo".

Each of the events that constitute this enlightening gala have been thoughtfully planned towards their eccentricity. As exciting and surprising that the events and their rules are, the rewards for participants are much more fulfilling. Along with gaining lifelong experience of participating in one of the most enriching events, participants have the opportunity to bag prizes worth 12 Lac Rupees!

Each year, more than 10,000 people discover more about themselves and their surroundings by associating with this revolution known as EXPLORE'17. This year , over the 25th and 26th March 2017 , talent from across the nation shall hail to dive in the sea of events ranging from dancing and singing to theaters and fine arts , from fashion shows to entertaining stars , from cooking and gaming to literary championships!

Chitkara University conducts and annual event called eXplore. Explore is a platform for each and every collage invited tocome and display there talents infront of the crowd. Many students from all over the city and the state come over to participate in event.

Explore fest is conducted for 2 days and both the days are full of entertainment. The eXplore fests first day comprises fo the dance performance by various colleges. The performance are based on daily struggles and some are for entertainment purposes. After all the perfomance a break is allotted for the students to go and refresh themselves at the college canteens. College canteenscomprise of 3 canteens named as SQUARE 1, TEA QUOTIENT, ARCHITUECTURE CANTEEN. They are a verygood place to talk and make new friends.

After the break then the dance competion will resume anmd then there will be the Dj Night or the concert by famous singing artists from all over the country.


EXPLORE DAY-1

eXplore day 1 witnessed many events which includes dance performace from many colleges across the country. The dance competition had many prices for the winning teams which were to be distributed the next day. The night of the first day had the Dj night which was from Dj Julia Bliss.












EXPLORE DAY-2

eXplore day 2 witnessed many events which included many dances which ended into prize distribution. The day 2 had many attraction with all of the students spending time which each other in parks and grounds. The students had a jolly time this eXplore and the night concluded with an elecrifying performance from the local singer Hardy Sandhu.
