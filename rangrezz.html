RANGREZZ-2016

National Theatre Fest - Rangrezz is the epitome of theatre. Serving as an abode to the artists and actors nationwide, it is the zenith of celebration. Each of the events of this exuberant fiesta have been outlined to promise its essence and credibility. With teams participating from all over the country, We aspire to offer the largest, grandest and the loudest stage to all the laudable and exemplary actors. The rangrezz was a very succesfull evengt whuch displayedmany talents from acroos the india. There was a good competion as well as a team spirit between the team which came here. The national theater fest gave the students to meet Mr. Pawan Malhotra, who had shown his capabilities in the field of acting and has a great hold in indian film industry. The students had the chance to witness the mesmorizing Dj candice from the Sunburn.. She played many tracks which compelled the students to jump on the beats. She played an electrifying performence for around an hour. The next day also the theater festival continued with street plays and theater actings. That night the chitkarians witnessed the indian rock band named The Local Train. They put out a stunning performance of indian rock music with some ofthere own albums and songs and the crowd really enjoyed the night. All in all the fest was conducted and managed efficiently and the audinces were happy to come and see the fest.
