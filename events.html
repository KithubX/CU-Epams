Events

The Chitkara University organizes many events every year. The events consist of the audiences and the managing team which helps the event to be great success.The events can be categorized in two ways

Firstly, the events which are conducted by the university for the welfare of the students or the staff.

Secondly, the events which are made and conduct by the students for fun. These events help to build the competition spirit and the friendship in the campus of the university.

The events are the main source of entertainment in the university campus and the colleges around the state.

The students also take the opportunity to increases their skills in various fields.
